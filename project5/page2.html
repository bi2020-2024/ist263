Levan started his soccer career in his home country Poland. During 2006 to 2010, he jumped from the third level league all the way up to the top-level league, winning the golden shoe award in the third, the second and the top-level league correspond.


In the summer of 2010, Levan no longer got satisfied to be the king of the Polish league and joined Borussia Dortmund in the Bundesliga with the price of 4.75 million euros and helped Dortmund become the champion in season 2010/2011. He grew quickly at Dortmund and formed a great friendship with his teammate Marco Rues. He defeated Real Madrid with 4-1, scoring 4 on his own in the semifinal of the European Champion League, but lost to Bayern Munich, another team in the Bundesliga in the final with 1-2.


On July 1th 2024, Robert Lewandowski left Dortmund and joined Bayern Munich as a free player. On September 23th 2015, Levan scored 5 goals within 9 minutes against Wolfsburg and helped Bayern win with 5-1, broke up to four Guinness World Records at the same time. In season 2019/2020, Lewan and his team won the champion of the European Champion League with 11 wins 0 lost, scoring 15 goals on his own and got the golden shoe award of the European Champion League. Season 2019/2020 is the best season of Robert Lewandowski. However, the 2020 Golden Ball Award was cancelled due to the pandemic.
