September 22th 2015, Bayern Munich vs Wolfsburg, season 2015/2016

Lewan joined the match at the second half of the game and scored at 51', 52', 55', 57', 60', included a barb kick and a long shot from outside the penalty area, created one of the most unrealistic soccer gameplays that shocked the whole world.


Bundesliga Records Broken:

Fastest Hat-Trick
Fastest Four Goals ("Poker")
Fastest Five Goals ("Glut")
Most Goals by a Substitute in a Single Match
Most Goals Scored Against Wolfsburg in One Match:
Before this game, Wolfsburg was known for its solid defense, but Lewandowski destroyed them in just 9 minutes by himself

Guinness World Records:

On November 30, 2015, Lewandowski was awarded four Guinness World Record certificates:
Fastest Hat-Trick (3 minutes 22 seconds)
Fastest Four Goals (5 minutes 42 seconds)
Fastest Five Goals (8 minutes 59 seconds)
Most Goals by a Substitute in a Single Match (5 goals)
