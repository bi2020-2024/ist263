The Legendary Game

Five Goals - Nine Minutes

September 22nd, 2015 — Bayern Munich vs Wolfsburg, season 2015/2016

Lewandowski entered in the second half and scored at the 51st, 52nd, 55th, 57th, and 60th minutes. His goals included a bicycle kick and a long shot, creating one of the most jaw-dropping performances in football history.

Bundesliga Records Broken:

- Fastest Hat-Trick
- Fastest Four Goals ("Poker")
- Fastest Five Goals ("Glut")
- Most Goals by a Substitute in a Single Match
- Most Goals Against Wolfsburg in One Match

Lewandowski demolished Wolfsburg's solid defense in just 9 minutes.

Guinness World Records (Awarded Nov 30, 2015):

- Fastest Hat-Trick (3 min 22 sec)
- Fastest Four Goals (5 min 42 sec)
- Fastest Five Goals (8 min 59 sec)
- Most Goals by a Substitute (5 goals)

Post-Match Reactions:

Pep Guardiola (Coach): "I've never seen anything like this! It's beyond explanation!"

Manuel Neuer (Goalkeeper): "It felt like Lewy was playing a video game. Unreal!"

Bundesliga Official: "ROBERT LEWANDOWSKI - 9 MINUTES, 5 GOALS, 4 RECORDS! Legendary!"

The Audience: "No edits needed — it was a live highlight reel!"
