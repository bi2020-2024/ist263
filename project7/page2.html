Career Path

The Beginning...

Levan started his soccer career in his home country Poland. From 2006 to 2010, he climbed from the third-tier league to the top-tier league, winning the golden shoe award in each division respectively.

Dortmund

In the summer of 2010, Levan decided to challenge himself further by joining Borussia Dortmund in the Bundesliga for 4.75 million euros. He helped Dortmund win the 2010/2011 championship, formed a strong partnership with Marco Reus, and famously scored 4 goals against Real Madrid in the UEFA Champions League semifinals. However, Dortmund lost the final to Bayern Munich.

Bayern-Munich

On July 1st, 2014, Lewandowski joined Bayern Munich as a free transfer. On September 23rd, 2015, he scored 5 goals in 9 minutes against Wolfsburg, breaking four Guinness World Records. In the 2019/2020 season, Bayern won the UEFA Champions League with 11 wins and 0 losses; Levan scored 15 goals and earned the Golden Shoe.
