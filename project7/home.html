Robert Lewandowski

Who He Is?

For the past two decades, when talking about the best soccer players, most people will think about Leno Messi and Cristiano Ronaldo. However, there's a player who is also brilliant but always gets ignored under the influence of Messi and Ronaldo. His name is Robert Lewandowski.


Robert Lewandowski, born 21 August 1988 in Warsaw, Poland, is a Polish professional footballer who plays as a forward. He currently plays for FC Barcelona in the Spanish La Liga. He is also the captain of the Polish national men's football team.


Robert Lewandowski is one of the top centre-forwards in world football nowadays. His shooting skill is extremely fantastic and with both feet balance, allowing him to score even with very limited space. His finishing ability is very comprehensive, especially for getting head ball. Levan's movement is extremely intelligent, able to accurately anticipate defensive gaps and be in the right position at the right time. He has superior physical fitness, which allows him to keep balance during sprint or fighting with the opponent defender. He can always keep the ball under his feet and create opportunities for other teammates by attracting major defense. He has a strong sense of team cooperation and chemical reaction with his teammates. He penalty shoot is one of the best in the history, hardly miss one. What's more, he is highly disciplined and trains hard, which is the reason why he is still competitive in the age of 35.


Overall, Lewandowski is a classic example of a modern, versatile centre-forward who can adapt to a variety of tactical systems and is a very threatening striker.
